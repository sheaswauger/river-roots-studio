There are very few rituals that acknowledge our visible grief, much less the hidden grief we experience unseen by others. Many of us are feeling the ripping and tearing of communal grief alongside our personal losses. Together we can count on the wild, unruliness of mourning the people and things that previously grounded us.

This workshop is a tender space that uses the healing of arts, ancient traditions, stories from quilts and the gifts of herbs to create bundles. These bundles aid in small repairs of loss giving witness to what you've carried.

Within the sanctuary of community, you'll be seen, held, and strengthened.
