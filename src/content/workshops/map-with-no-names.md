There is no map left for us when we stand in the ashes of what was. Some ruins are invisible, sometimes even from ourselves as we attempt to function as though our world had not changed. We didn't want this living relationship with loss and sorrow.

When in uncharted territory deep healing can occur within creativity and kindred souls. It is not a simple fix for the rending, yet a portal opens to help us translate the wordless parts of ourselves that don't make sense.

This workshop gives you a sanctuary alongside the gifts of blessings, phoenix ashes, and a wailing wall. Your heart will have a witness and permission to be lighter.
