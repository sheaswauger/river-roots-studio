We carry the northern lights within us; they are signs to our self connection. We see the moving colors across our inner sky when our skin does this phenomenal thing… they call goosebumps. These uncontrollable acts of presence happen when our hearts surpass our brain, and our gut surpasses our heart. We can't think them into being, but we can "train" for them like Andrea Gibson did. They paid the cost of tearing the caution tape off their life.

It seems that only then can the movie of our past be released into color. I've accepted the challenge of breaking their world record in Goosebumps. My way is by "repurposing wonder." Want to come along?

This workshop opens sometime in September.
