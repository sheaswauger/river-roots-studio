The Thread workshop is a way to connect to those quieter voices of your inner knowing and the body's wisdom of: "my gut told me." This foraging of threads is a one-of-a-kind workshop experience. We'll use a practice of writing with our non-dominant hand revealing truths that our linear brain can't fathom. Weaving various images and soulful questions, paper and pen take on an inner alchemy and depth. You'll reveal threads running through your life that are ready to be seen in all their color and discover secret longings that you didn't know you had.
