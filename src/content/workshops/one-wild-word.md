This workshop is for a new beginning, or a sudden transition causing anxiety and all the feelings. When you're not able to walk the Camino De Santiago for clarity or even get a few quiet minutes alone behind the bathroom door, there is hope.

A wild word is a strong and stubborn enough container to: renew faltering hope, send bits of wisdom, and grant the graces of belly-laughs. Finding one wild word is a ritual I use to guide me through uncertain times, or the beginning of the next year.

Together we'll seek the word that shimmers while others remain the color of ink. Within Lectio Divina, harvesting the presence of beauty and listening to your life your wild word will disclose itself to you, or at least hint at a theme.
