This magical and highly tactile workshop makes you feel like you've just returned from a forest bath, or gives you an odd desire to become a botanist or poet. In connecting with nature by weaving earth looms we move closer to Mary Oliver's instructions for living a life.

> Pay Attention.
> Be Astonished.
> Tell about it.

Immerse yourself in a mini-nature retreat regardless of the weather and be touched by the gentle power of poetry. Dress casual.
