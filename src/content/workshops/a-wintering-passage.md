If the fallowness of winter interrupts the rhythms of your life. If you're trying to make peace with the darker days and impolite temperatures, granting yourself a pause may nourish you enough to find the gifts in even this crossing. I confess the word "wintering" leaves my tongue with a bitter taste. If bodies sync with nature's seasons, mine syncs with gardening and the smell of BBQ, so I made this for those like me.

This workshop involves nature, writing, and making a winter bundle. You may open your bundle come spring, bury it, or burn it. Feel free to bring a small symbol to tuck inside your bundle or add later for any hopes frozen in this season.
