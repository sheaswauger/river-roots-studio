If only our hearts had navigational systems, especially when traversing scary or unknown territory. Maybe they do. In bewildering moments finding the answer to the next right direction may count on us asking the right questions. Finding these breadcrumbs awakens our curiosity and opens kaleidoscope possibilities.

This is a wildly fun way to receive whispered directions from ordinary objects. With a "compass vessel," insightful writing and tiny message bearers you'll find keys you didn't know you held and some interesting messages that may surprise you.
